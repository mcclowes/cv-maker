# Senior Backend Engineer — Acme

## About the role

Acme is hiring a Senior Backend Engineer to join our platform team. You'll own
core services that power our public API, working closely with product and
infrastructure to keep them fast, reliable, and easy to build on.

## What you'll do

- Design, build, and operate backend services in Node.js / TypeScript.
- Evolve our public REST and GraphQL APIs used by hundreds of integration partners.
- Improve observability, on-call, and incident response across the platform.
- Mentor engineers and raise the bar on code review and testing.

## What we're looking for

- 5+ years of backend or full-stack engineering experience.
- Strong with Node.js, TypeScript, and relational databases (PostgreSQL).
- Experience designing and versioning public APIs.
- A track record of shipping reliable, well-tested production systems.
